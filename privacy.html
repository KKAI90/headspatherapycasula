Privacy Policy

AdManager  ·  Last updated: May 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AdManager ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application and services, including our integration with the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. About AdManager

AdManager is a content management and advertising tool that allows users to connect their TikTok accounts to publish, schedule, and manage video content through the TikTok Content Posting API. AdManager is operated independently and is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance Ltd.

2. Information We Collect

When you use AdManager, we may collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authenticate via TikTok OAuth, we receive your TikTok user ID, display name, and profile picture (as permitted by the scopes you authorize).
  • Content Data: Videos, captions, and publishing settings you submit through AdManager for posting to TikTok.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with AdManager, including pages viewed, actions taken, and timestamps.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies necessary for the service to function.

3. How We Use Your Information

AdManager uses the information collected to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session via TikTok OAuth 2.0.
  • Publish content to TikTok on your behalf when you explicitly request it.
  • Display your account details and publishing history within AdManager.
  • Improve and optimize the functionality of AdManager.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support requests.

4. TikTok API Data Usage

AdManager integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API. By connecting your TikTok account, you authorize AdManager to:

  • Access your basic TikTok profile information (user.info.basic).
  • Publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf (video.publish).

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide AdManager's services. We do not access your TikTok messages, contacts, or any data beyond the authorized scopes. You can revoke AdManager's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

AdManager does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate AdManager (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your AdManager account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to revoke app access when no longer needed.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to delete your data.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@admanager.app.

9. Cookies

AdManager uses essential cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of AdManager.

10. Children's Privacy

AdManager is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of AdManager after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or AdManager's data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@admanager.app
  • Website: https://kkai90.github.io/admanager/
